SWIFT security matters for your Business

SWIFT made the Customer Security Program (CSP) Audit mandatory from July 2020 as partof strengthening the security of the SWIFT ecosystem. We are a SWIFT Authorized CSP assessment provider who can assist your organization in achieving CSP compliance andValidate your compliance. We can assist you with:

  • Gap analysis with that of your current practice and CSP requirement’s Compliance
  • Preparing Customer Security Controls Framework (CSCF) implementation roadmap
  • Implementing Customer Security Controls Framework (CSCF)
  • Validating your organization’s SWIFT CSP compliance
