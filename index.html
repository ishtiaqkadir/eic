About Us

Choose your organization security with us.

Why Our Service is So Popular With organization

With an aim to provide the best cutting edge information security services to its valued clients, Enterprise InfoSec Consultants (EIC) started its journey in 2016. Initially, EIC was engaged with IT Audit and Cyber Security Consultancy services to its clients. in the past few years, EIC has established a strong footprint in ISO standard implementation in different domains and industries. At present, we are the only Bangladeshi company to become SWIFT Cyber Security Service provider and SWIFT CSP Assessment Provider.

we provide the following services including certification:

  • SWIFT CSP Audit
  • SWIFT Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing
  • Consultancy on Control Implementation for SWIFT
  • We are also PCI QSA Company, a certifying body for Payment card security compliance validation (PCI DSS Certification).
  • EIC is now not only a trusted name in Bangladesh only, we have clients in the USA and Sri Lanka.
