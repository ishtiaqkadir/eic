Consultancy Company

Enterprise InfoSec Consultants (EIC) started with an aim to provide the best cutting edge information security services to its valued clients. EIC started its journey on 2016. Initially EIC was engaged with IT Audit and Cyber Security Consultancy services to its clients. The knowledge gained over the years for protecting information assets from the hacker attacks, fueled our evolution into exploring more aspects of information security compliance, testing and information system governance engagements.

With the increasing needs for our valued clients, we have expanded our services and on boarded different cyber security solutions as well as training programs. Today, EIC provides professional consulting services in compliance auditing, such as PCI DSS, ISO/IEC 27001, SWIFT CSP Audit, GDPR and much more. Furthermore, we offer the full range of testing services to supplement these compliance endeavors as well as information security governance services that help our customers build and maintain resilient IT infrastructures and enable them to offer secure services manned by qualified staff.
